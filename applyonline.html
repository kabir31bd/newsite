How to Apply

1. Complete the Registration Form on this page and submit.

2. Scan and send a high-quality copy of the applicant’s academic record or transcript, to admission@queenscanada.com, with a subject “Application_yourfirstname_yourlastname”.

3. Once the transcript is sent, an Admissions Officer will review and contact you to complete your application.
