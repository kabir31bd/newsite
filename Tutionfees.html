Refund Policy

A tuition refund can only be issued if the student’s Study Permit Application has been rejected by Citizenship and Immigration Canada; tuition refunds will not be issued under any other circumstances, including but not limited to the following: a) The student chooses to withdraw from Queen’s Elite Academy voluntarily for any reason. b) The student is found in violation of school regulations or discipline policy and asked to withdraw from the school by the principal. c) False medical information was given and/or medical conditions were not disclosed.

To apply for a refund, the student must provide: a) A Letter of Explanation signed by the student and parent/guardian. b) The original Letter of Rejection from Citizenship and Immigration Canada. c) The original Official Letter of Acceptance from Queen’s Elite Academy. d) The receipt of tuition payment.

QUEEN’S ELITE ACADEMY RESERVES THE RIGHTS OF FINAL EXPLANATION FOR THE CONTENT IN THIS DOCUMENT AND TO CHANGE THE REFUND POLICY AT ANY TIME WITH OR WITHOUT PRIOR NOTICE. PLEASE CONTACT US IF YOU HAVE ANY QUESTIONS.
