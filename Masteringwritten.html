About US

About US

Give yourself or your child a good and affordable English and Islamic foundational education at Queens Academy enabling them to excel in their future endeavours.

  • A world class curriculum, with defined milestones and learning objectives to provide a clear path to progression.
  • Strong conceptual foundations built through experiential and activity based learning.
  • Focus on skills development without compromising academic outcomes to ensure future success in all aspects of a life.
  • A practical approach to education, providing students with a real-world context to their learning journey.
  • Adult individual and Parents can provide themselves and their children a world-class education with peace of mind, and enjoy being a part of every step of their own career life and their child’s learning journey respectively.

About the founder

Ms. Madam Ayesha Noor

(Previously, Lana J. Frost)

Founding Principal and CEO

Educated in the UK, Madam Ayesha is an experienced education management professional, having held the post of Director of Studies and Principal at many different English medium schools and colleges from eu to Indian subcontinent to Australia New Zealand. Madam Ayesha is a mother of two children.

Madam Ayesha feels passionately about English teaching, mental health and in depth authentic foundational Islamic learning for adults and teen ages and for early childhood development as she believes that getting the learning right at the start allows students to be successful throughout their schooling and later in life. She also has experience with curriculum development and teachers training, including conducting teacher training workshops in Indian subcontinent. She is a published author of several thesises and poetry books and teacher's resource materials.

Madam Ayesha believes that Queens Academy is a nation building exercise to empower the youth of Bangladesh by providing highest quality, affordable education. Her vision to build an education network that is based on systems and process and solid teacher training to ensure students receive a consistent level of quality education focused on skills development.
