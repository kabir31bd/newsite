As-salamu Alaykum

Welcome to Daar e aysha, a progressive, successful and deeply caring establishment where our aim is to provide an excellent academic education within the structure of the national syllabus. At the same time, however, we also strive to help each and every individual girl to develop her own character and achieve her personal potential and ambitions through a deep appreciation of her deen (Islam). Supported by our own Tarbiyah programme we promote Islamic morals and manners for all our students on their journey to becoming valued members of society.

Our Core Values
